These icons were designed to highlight the diverse reasons to explore Greece.

This icon symbolizes a family holiday, focusing on travel, shared adventures, and the joy of exploring new destinations together.

Wine tourism celebrates the exploration of vineyards, fine wines, and the timeless art of winemaking.

Agrotourism

Agrotourism highlights the charm of rural life, traditional farming, and the natural beauty of the countryside.

Alternative Tourism

Alternative tourism focuses on unique travel experiences, promoting sustainability and exploring off-the-beaten paths.

Business Tourism

Honeymoon

Honeymoon tourism captures romantic getaways and unforgettable moments in stunning destinations.
