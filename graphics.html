About the Project

I decided to showcase the creative work from my latest project, where I not only contributed as a full-stack developer but also took responsibility for designing all the graphical elements.

The project is a travel-guide e-booking platform focused on Greece. For the logo, I aimed to reflect Greece as simply and elegantly as possible. The directive for the icons was to ensure they remained clean, minimalistic, and monochromatic, aligning with the overall aesthetic vision.

All rights to the project and its elements belong to Net and Design.
