About the Project

I decided to showcase the creative work from my latest project, where I not only contributed as a full-stack developer but also took responsibility for designing all the graphical elements. The project is a travel-guide e-booking platform focused on Greece, a land of unmatched beauty and rich cultural heritage.

For the logo, I aimed to reflect Greece as simply and elegantly as possible. The directive for the icons was to ensure they remained clean, minimalistic, and monochromatic, aligning with the overall aesthetic vision.
