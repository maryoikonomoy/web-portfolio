About the Project

I decided to showcase the creative work from my latest project, where I not only contributed as a full-stack developer but also took responsibility for designing all the graphical elements.

The project is a travel-guide e-booking platform focused on Greece. For the logo, I aimed to reflect Greece as simply and elegantly as possible. The directive for the icons was to ensure they remained clean, minimalistic, and monochromatic, aligning with the overall aesthetic vision.

Tourism Essentials

Explore 11 alternative tourism icons to enhance your travel planning and discover new adventures.

Family holiday

This icon was created to visually represent the concept of a family holiday, capturing the essence of togetherness, relaxation, and memorable adventures shared among loved ones.

Wine Tourism

This icon was designed to represent wine tourism, celebrating the experience of exploring vineyards, savoring fine wines, and immersing oneself in the culture and tradition of winemaking

Agrotourism

This icon was created to represent agritourism in Greece, showcasing the unique experience of connecting with rural life, exploring traditional farming practices, and embracing the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the countryside.

Alternative Tourism

This icon represents alternative tourism, highlighting unique and unconventional travel experiences that focus on sustainability, cultural immersion, and exploring less-traveled paths

Business Tourism

This icon represents business travel, focusing on the professional journeys made for meetings, conferences, and networking, blending efficiency and purpose with seamless mobility.

Gastronomy Tourism

This icon symbolizes gastronomy tourism, celebrating the art of exploring diverse culinary traditions, savoring local flavors, and immersing oneself in the cultural heritage of food and cuisine.

Eco Tourism

This icon represents eco-tourism, emphasizing sustainable travel that connects with nature, promotes environmental conservation, and encourages responsible exploration of natural ecosystems.

Religious Tourism

This icon represents religious tourism, reflecting journeys of faith, spiritual exploration, and visits to sacred sites and landmarks that hold cultural and religious significance.

Island Hopping

This icon represents island hopping, capturing the essence of exploring multiple islands, experiencing their unique beauty, and immersing yourself in the diverse cultures and landscapes that each destination offers

Accessible Tourism

This icon represents accessible tourism, emphasizing inclusivity and the creation of travel experiences that are designed to accommodate people of all abilities, ensuring everyone can explore and enjoy the world comfortably

Honeymoon

This icon represents honeymoon tourism, celebrating the joy of romantic getaways and the beginning of a lifelong journey together, set against breathtaking destinations designed for unforgettable memories
